Dairyland partnership 

Dairyland has been in the market for over 25 years and is currently the market leader in the Ice cream category with a 56% market share. Starting off as an ice cream company, Dairyland has won the hearts of millions of consumers in Kenya and regionally because of their commitment to quality and innovation. The brand has over time diversified their range to include ambient and Chilled Categories; Real Milk Chocolates, Baking Compounds and Real Fruit Yogurts.

To be at par with international standards, Dairyland has invested heavily in globally recognized food certification systems (FSSC 22000, HACCP, GMP). Dairyland has a dedicated team of technical experts who work tirelessly to monitor, evaluate, and continuously improve its product and processes, adhering to local and international regulations. We believe that delivering safe and high-quality products is not just a responsibility, but a fundamental part of our mission to provide our customers with products they can trust and enjoy with confidence.

The most recent product innovation was the introduction of the Non-Dairy vegan ice cream in mid-2023 targeted to the growing lactose intolerant community in the country and region. The digital campaign dubbed Dairy- Free Dream won an accolade at the MSK Awards for the Best Digital campaign of 2023. We partnered with Java on a digital campaign to launch the first ever vegan milkshake menu which is currently available in over 10 Java branches which is a big step towards ensuring inclusivity within quick service restaurants and beyond.
